Museums in the Netherlands, life of a museum girl!

The Netherlands offers a museumkaart with fee of around 70 euros per year allowing you to visit hundreds of museums there, such a big deal!!! As a museum girl, I go for it. This is one of lots of things that I wish I know earlier after arrivals to this country. Anyway, in this post, I'm gonna list out and review where I have been to. Also, it would be great if you can send me message on where I should go if you know of somewhere and I haven't been there yet.

  1. Amsterdam (Rijk Museum, Van Gogh Museum, Anne Frank Huis)
  2. Den Haag (Maurithuis, Rijksmuseum de Gevangenpoort)
  3. Delft (Museum Prinsenhof Delft, Royal Delft)
  4. Utrecht (Spoorweg Museum, Kasteel De Haar)
  5. Others (Museum Hoorn-Medemblik, Arnhem Openluchtmuseum, Mondriaanhuis Amersfoort)
  6. and more...
