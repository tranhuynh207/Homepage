Market Segmentation

This project uses dataset from a retail store to identify customer groups for marketing strategy planning. Below is the specific objectives:

  1. Identify and visualize customer profiles in general, and by gender
  2. Use k-mean cluster analysis to divide customers groups and depict profiles based on demographic information
  3. Suggest business/marketing strategies


Keywords: Pandas, Seaborn, Matplotlib, KMean, Machine Learning
