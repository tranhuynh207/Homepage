About me

Hi, my name is Tran. I hold a master's degree in Economics and Development with experience working for both non-profit and private sectors.

I am passionate about working with organizations that want to make effective use of their data. This can be from the first ideas around what you want to measure and how to capture data accurately, through data preparation, and analysis, to presentation delivery with impactful insights.

Coming from social research background, I have theoretical and practical knowledge of questionnaire design and impact assessment. I have data collection and management experience with Kobo Toolbox, Qualtrics, and EpiLab. As a self-taught data analyst, I have strong data preparation, exploration, and visualization knowledge with SQL, Tableau, R, and Python.
