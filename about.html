About me

Hello, I am Tran, born and raised in a beautiful country in Asia, Vietnam, while I currently belong to the cozy city Amersfoort in Holland. I feel grateful that I live in a very peaceful place but also the central point of Holland; so, rolling around the country is super convenient, which is great for a travel-lover like me.
