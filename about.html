About me

Hi, I am Tran, born and raised in a beautiful country of Asia, Vietnam, while I currently belong to the cozy city named Amersfoort in the Netherlands.

For me, the year of 2022 marks a watershed moment: four years living in four different countries in different continents (COVID-19 could NOT keep me from moving!!!). It is not just about seeing the world; it is about discovering myself, evolving my life perspective, and changing my approach to life.

I have a master's degree in Economics and Development and have worked as a social science researcher since I was in university. After working in research for a while, I decided to specialize on data analysis. As a first step toward breaking into the field of data analytics and business intelligence, I spent my time taking online courses. The more time I spent on it, the more excited I became about how powerful data tools can be used to study the patterns behind people, life, and the universe; this provided me with all the motivation and determination I could possibly need to take on challenges in order to pursue this new career path.

Coming from a background in social science research, I have a strong analytical mindset, as well as theoretical and practical knowledge and experience with questionnaire design and impact assessment. I've worked with Kobo Toolbox, Qualtrics, and EpiLab to collect and manage data. I also have a solid understanding of SQL, Tableau, R, and Python for data preparation, exploration, and visualization. This website is intended to offer you a glimpse of my abilities.

But I'm not just a data nerd!

As a communicator, I can precisely express my thoughts and understand those of others thanks to my experience working and living with international communities.

As a team member, I am accountable, effective, and willing to go above and beyond to get the job done.

As a person, I am easygoing, enthusiastic, curious, and fun (somehow).

As a member of society, I am always willing to do volunteer work to help the community in any way I can.

What I care and love: Myself; My family; Good friends; Data; Natures; Sustainability; Books; Arts; and my Ukulele
