About me

Hi, I am Tran, born and raised in a beautiful country in Asia, Vietnam, while I currently belong to the cozy city Amersfoort in Holland.

The year of 2022 marks a significant milestone for me: 4 years living in 4 different countries (COVID-19 could NOT stop me from moving !!!). It is much more than just seeing different corners of the world, it is about finding myself, changing my life perspective and my ideas of living.

I hold a master's degree in Economics and Development with experience working as a social researcher since I was at university. After a while working in research, I decided to make my career focusing more on data. I spent my time following online courses as the first step to break into data analytics and business intelligence field. The more time I have spent on it, the more I become excited about how powerful data tools can be used to study the patterns behind people, life, and the universe; this provided me with all the motivation and determination I could need to take challenges to pursue this new career path.

Coming from a social research background, I have a strong analytical mindset with theoretical and practical knowledge and experience of questionnaire design and impact assessment. I have data collection and management experience with Kobo Toolbox, Qualtrics, and EpiLab. As a self-taught data analyst, I have solid understanding of data preparation, exploration, and visualization knowledge with SQL, Tableau, R, and Python. This website aims to show you a glimpse of my abilities.

But I am not just a data passionate!

As a communicator, throughout my experience living in different countries, and working with different people, I can precisely express my thoughts and understand those of others.

As a team member, I am responsible, effective and willing to do anything to make the work done.

As a person in life, I am easy-going, enthusiastic, curious, and fun (somehow).

As a member of society, I am always willing to do volunteer work to contribute whatever I can to the community.

What I care and love: Myself; My family; Good friends; Data; Natures; Sustainability; Books; Arts; and my Ukulele
